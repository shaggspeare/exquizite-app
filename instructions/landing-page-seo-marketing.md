## FAQ Section (SEO-Optimized)

### H2: Frequently Asked Questions

#### What languages does Exquizite support?

Exquizite supports any language pair you choose. Configure your native and target languages during setup to start learning immediately.

#### Is Exquizite free?

Yes, you can create unlimited vocabulary sets and access all study modes for free. Start learning today without any payment required.

#### How does the AI suggestion feature work?

Simply enter a topic (like "cooking," "travel," or "business"), and our AI generates relevant vocabulary with accurate translations in seconds, saving you hours of manual work.

#### Can I use Exquizite offline?

Your saved sets are available offline once downloaded to your device, so you can study anywhere, anytime.

#### Can I share my vocabulary sets?

Absolutely! Share sets with friends, classmates, or study groups via a simple share link. Perfect for collaborative learning.

#### What study modes are available?

Exquizite offers four engaging study modes:

- **Flashcards** - Classic flip-and-learn
- **Quiz** - Multiple choice challenges
- **Match** - Fast-paced word matching
- **Fill in the Blank** - Context-based learning

#### How does progress tracking work?

Track your learning streak, total words studied, and number of sets completed. Visual stats keep you motivated and show your improvement over time.

#### Can teachers use Exquizite for their classes?

Yes! Teachers can create vocabulary sets for students and share them instantly. It's a great way to provide engaging study materials.

## Launch Strategy

1. Create landing page with email signup

### Launch Day

1. Post on Product Hunt
2. Submit to app directories
